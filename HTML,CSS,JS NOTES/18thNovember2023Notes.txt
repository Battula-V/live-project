Assignment Operators:
===================
	Operator		Meaning
	========		=======
	      =		Normal Assignment
	   a+=b;		a=a+b;
	   a-=b;		a=a-b;
	   a*=b;		a=a*b;
	   a/=b;		a=a/b;
	   a%=b;		a=a%b;
	   a**=b;		a=a**b;
	   a&=b;		a=a&b;
	   a|=b;		a=a|b;
	   a^=b;		a=a^b;
	   a<<=b;		a=a<<b;
	   a>>=b;		a=a>>b;

Conditional Operators:
===================
It is a ternary operator & it operates on three operands.

Syntax:
======
	operand1 ? operand2 : operand3

Example:
========
<html>
<body>
<script>
let a=5, b=3;
let c=(a>b)?a:b;
document.write(c);
</script>
</body>
</html>

Unary Plus & Unary Minus Operators:
===============================
	Operator		Meaning
	========		=======
	       +		Positive
	       - 		Negative

Example:
========
<html>
<body>
<script>
let a=-5;
let b=-a;
document.write(b);
</script>
</body>
