Gradient properties:
=================
Gradient properties are used to display transitions 
between two or more colors.

There are three types of gradients:
1) Linear Gradients
2) Radial Gradients
3) Conic Gradients

1) Linear Gradients Property Values:
===============================
1) linear-gradient(red, yellow);=> Top to Bottom
2) linear-gradient(orange, white, green); => Top to Bottom
3) linear-gradient(to right, red, yellow); => Left to Right
4) linear-gradient(to right, orange, white, green); => Left
				to Right
5) linear-gradient(to bottom right, red, yellow);
	=> Top Left to Bottom Right
6) linear-gradient(to bottom right, orange, white, green);
	=> Top Left to Bottom Right
7) linear-gradient(to bottom right, red, green, blue, 
orange, cyan, purple, yellow);
8) repeating-linear-gradient(orange 10%, white 20%,
green 30%);

2) Radial Gradients Property Values:
===============================
1) radial-gradient(red, yellow);
2) radial-gradient(orange, white, green);
3) repeating-radial-gradient(red 10%, yellow 20%);
4) repeating-radial-gradient(orange 10%, white 20%,
			green 30%);

3) Conic Gradients Property Values:
===============================
1) conic-gradient(red, yellow);
2) conic-gradient(orange, white, green);
3) repeating-conic-gradient(red 10%, yellow 20%);
4) repeating-conic-gradient(orange 10%, white 20%,
			green 30%);
